Our Services

Mobile Apps

One of the most rapidly growing markets

The smartphone market is one of the most rapidly growing markets out there, that is why any business, not wanting to be left behind, needs to extend its services towards the mobile customer.

Mobile applications will allow you to take advantage of new smartphone technologies and provide the user with information he or she is looking for.

Mobile Games

The new way of promoting your business

The user will not only be having fun playing the game, but will also recommends it for his or her friends, who are all potential new customers.

Positive emotions received from the games will be associated with your company, boosting it's image and helping it develop a solid consumer base.

Web Apps

Mobile applications which run in a browser

The biggest advantage of Web Apps is universality as one solution will fit any mobile devices which supports HTML5 technology: iOS, Android and BlackBerry.

The user will not be required to perform any additional installations or updates in order to run our applications.

Gamification

Proccess of enhancing your application

Inserting game elements into the workflow of a simple application, has the potential of greatly increasing the user base, time spent on and, of course, the very value of the application itself.

A gamified application can implement a larger variety of business models, such as testing a new product for a special prizes.

Interactive Presentations

A different way of presenting your products

It is a mini application installed into customer smartphone. It is dedicated to introduce a new product or service in easy and interesting way.

During every presentation it is possible to collect statistical data and use it later for future presentations.
